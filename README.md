this is an open broadcast network, community-driven, free tv & radio directory.

> [!NOTE]
> **disclaimer:** fonseware-cable is not a real physical cable service and is not meant to replace your actual cable or satellite television provider. it is simply the brand name of this community-driven iptv service.

the goal is to fill **200 channels** with original, independent, 24/7 (preferable) broadcast content. university television networks, community channels, and generic independent broadcasts are highly encouraged.

> [!WARNING]
> **content warning & parental controls:** this playlist is community-driven and may contain adult, nsfw, or unrated content. we strongly encourage users to use a playlist manager or a media player with parental controls (such as the channel management and locking features in kodi) to restrict access to certain channels if minors use the device.
>
> we are not a piracy index. do not submit iptv-org or similar links. do not submit pirated sports streams. they will be rejected, and you will be blocked. do not submit streams you found on the internet.
